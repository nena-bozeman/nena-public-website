The **Neighborhood Conservation Overlay District (NCOD)** is a city zoning overlay created in 1991 to conserve neighborhood character and protect historic properties. Bozeman is updating **NCOD design guidelines** and related historic preservation policies with consultant-led outreach.

NENA tracks public meetings, neighborhood walks, and **Inter-Neighborhood Council** votes that affect the NCOD. Related news appears below; older posts document the 2018 review round.

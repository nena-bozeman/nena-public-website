The Traffic Calming Committee advocates for safer streets throughout the Northeast Neighborhood. We work with the City of Bozeman Traffic Engineering department to identify problem areas and implement solutions.

## Focus Areas

- Peach Street speed reduction
- Crosswalk improvements on N Rouse Ave
- Bicycle lane connectivity
- Pedestrian safety near Bozeman High School

## Recent Wins

- New pedestrian crossing signal at N Bozeman & Peach
- Speed feedback signs installed on Peach Street

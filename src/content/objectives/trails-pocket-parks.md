The Trails & Parks Committee focuses on expanding recreational opportunities within the Northeast Neighborhood. We advocate for new trails, maintain existing ones, and work to create small pocket parks on underutilized parcels.

## Current Projects

- Northeast Neighborhood to Story Mill trail connection
- Pocket park development at N Rouse & Oak
- Trail maintenance and wayfinding signage

## How to Help

Join a trail maintenance workday or attend our committee meetings (first Tuesday of each month at Beall Park).

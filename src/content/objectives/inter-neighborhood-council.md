The **Inter-Neighborhood Council (INC)** brings together representatives from recognized neighborhood associations each month. NENA’s INC representative reports back to the board and, when needed, **surveys neighbors** for a quorum before voting on behalf of Northeast on city issues — including **UDC** updates, **NCOD** interim zoning, and other regional topics.

INC is a City of Bozeman program, not a NENA committee, but it is one of the main channels where neighborhood associations speak together.

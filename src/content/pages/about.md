title: "About NENA"
---

The Northeast Neighborhood Association (NENA) represents the interests and community of the Northeast Neighborhood in Bozeman, Montana. We are a volunteer-driven organization dedicated to maintaining the character, livability, and community spirit of our neighborhood.

## Our Mission

To preserve and enhance the quality of life in the Northeast Neighborhood through community engagement, advocacy, and connection.

## History

NENA was founded in 1985 and has been a voice for Northeast Bozeman residents ever since. We work closely with the City of Bozeman on planning decisions that affect our neighborhood and host regular community events to bring neighbors together.

Creekside Park is a city park at **North Rouse Avenue** and **East Lamme Street** on the south side of the Northeast Neighborhood, near Bozeman Creek and the Rouse Avenue trail corridor.

The park sits along connections NENA has supported between neighborhood streets, Rouse Avenue, and the broader city trail network — including the [Front Street pathway](/news/2021-12-10-front-street-connector-trail) linking the neighborhood to Story Mill Community Park and paths farther east.

See the [City of Bozeman park listing](https://www.bozeman.net/Home/Components/FacilityDirectory/FacilityDirectory/220/38) for amenities, or [find the park on Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/BJFNiZpuK5ifRHRj9).

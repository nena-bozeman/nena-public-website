---
title: "Northeast Renaissance: Craft Beverage and Food Scene Emerges"
year: 2010
decade: 2010
category: "community"
---

The 2010s brought a wave of new small businesses to the Northeast Neighborhood, particularly craft breweries, bakeries, and coffee roasters. This "Northeast Renaissance" transformed the neighborhood into one of Bozeman's most vibrant commercial and residential areas while preserving much of its historic character.

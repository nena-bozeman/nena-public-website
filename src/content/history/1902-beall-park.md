---
title: "Beall Park Established"
year: 1902
decade: 1900
category: "landmark"
---

Beall Park, the heart of the Northeast Neighborhood, was established as a public park and recreation area. Named for a prominent early Bozeman family, the park has served as a gathering place for neighborhood residents for over a century. The Carnegie-funded library nearby cemented the area as Bozeman's cultural center.

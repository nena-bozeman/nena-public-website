Beall Park, the heart of the Northeast Neighborhood, was established as a public park and recreation area. Named for a prominent early Bozeman family, the park has served as a gathering place for neighborhood residents for over a century. The Carnegie-funded library nearby cemented the area as Bozeman's cultural center. See the [Beall Park place listing](/places/beall-park) and the [1927 community center](/history/1987-beall-park-community-center-added-to-the-national-register-of-historic-places) at the park.

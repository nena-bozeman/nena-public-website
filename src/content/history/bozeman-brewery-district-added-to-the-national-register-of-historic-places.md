**Bozeman Brewery Historic District**

The Bozeman Brewery Historic District is composed of five historic buildings that are directly associated with the Julius Lehrkind family and the family-owned-and-operated Bozeman Brewery business.



Learn more at the [Gallatin History Museum - Welcome to the Ring of Progress exhibit](https://www.gallatinhistorymuseum.org/welcomeringofchange)


See the original document for the [National Park Service historic record](https://npgallery.nps.gov/NRHP/GetAsset/48d22b23-cebc-49c1-b2ab-6ba07c82dd6b).

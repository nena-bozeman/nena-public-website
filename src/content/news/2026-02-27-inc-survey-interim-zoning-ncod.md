At its **March 12, 2026** meeting, the **Inter-Neighborhood Council (INC)** planned to discuss an **interim zoning ordinance** related to the **Neighborhood Conservation Overlay District (NCOD)**—including potentially limiting demolition and new applications over a stated size threshold until updated historic preservation code, local landmark program, and NCOD design guidelines are adopted.

Neighbors were asked to complete a short **survey** so NENA’s INC representative could report back with adequate participation (a quorum of responses was requested). The original message linked background reading on the topic and offered a contact for questions.

**Survey:** [INC neighborhood survey](https://www.surveymonkey.com/r/CP2H3K6) (deadline referenced in the email was **March 5, 2026**).  
**Background:** [The Case for Interim Zoning](https://www.nenabozeman.org/objectives/udc/interim-zoning-2026) (legacy NENA site).

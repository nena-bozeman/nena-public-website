**Natural Lawn Care Tips**

  
NENA Residents are encouraged to take a more natural approach, ditching chemicals and fast-release fertilizers in favor of organic food sources and life-first lawn care management. If you want to cultivate healthy soils in your yard, adopt these three key management practices: 1) Mow tall, 2) water deeply and infrequently, and 3) always use natural fertilizers  to create deep healthy root systems, promote beneficial soil biology, store more soil carbon, and increase water holding capacity.

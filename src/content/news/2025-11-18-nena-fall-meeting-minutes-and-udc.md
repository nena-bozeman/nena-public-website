About **55 neighbors** attended the bi-annual **fall meeting** (great potluck). Minutes were published on the legacy meetings hub (see **[Fall 2025 neighborhood meeting](/our-work/nena-meetings/2025-fall)** on this site for the migrated Word file).

The email emphasized that **public comment on the UDC** still needed to be **specific**—ideally proposed text edits—and pointed readers to **[engage.bozeman.net/udc](https://engage.bozeman.net/udc)** with draft documents, edit summaries, zoning maps, and the current **[Chapter 38](https://library.municode.com/mt/bozeman/codes/code_of_ordinances?nodeId=PTIICOOR_CH38UNDECO)**. It also noted Commission hearings on the project timeline.

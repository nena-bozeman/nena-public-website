Potential for another "pocket park" in the neighborhood

* * *

There may be an opportunity to preserve and enjoy a section of Bozeman Creek’s west bank north of Tamarack St. where  Church Street continues north and intersects with Juniper Street. The city owns the majority of the land that used to be a Brewery Spur Rail Line. Kenyon Noble owns a sliver of land immediately adjacent to the creek on its west shore. This space is maintained as an unofficial Pocket Park. The park features mature Willow Trees, large stones, hand cut benches, and Bozeman Creek. NENA is communicating with adjacent landowners, the City of Bozeman, GVLT, Upper Missouri Water Keeper, and the Gallatin Watershed Council. Author - Reno Walsh

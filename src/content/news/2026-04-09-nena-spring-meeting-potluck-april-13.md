Neighbors were invited to the **NENA Spring Meeting and Potluck** on **Monday, April 13, 2026**, at the City’s **Public Safety Center**, **Community Room** (**901 N. Rouse**). Potluck at **5:30 p.m.**, meeting at **6:00 p.m.** Bring a dish to share and your own plate and utensils if you can.

The agenda included board officer elections (President and Vice President), a treasurer report, and presentations on the City Landmark Program, Inter-Neighborhood Council interim zoning, Bozeman’s Water Conservation Program, Visioning Bozeman Creek, and a recap of the March 31 Pizza + Story Night.

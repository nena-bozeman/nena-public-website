The City of Bozeman is updating its **Integrated Water Resources Plan (IWRP)** to plan for a reliable water supply. Community feedback was requested via a brief survey.

The email also noted the **boulevard tree cost-share program** opening **Monday, March 16, 2026**, through the Forestry Division (boulevard planting with shared cost and City support).

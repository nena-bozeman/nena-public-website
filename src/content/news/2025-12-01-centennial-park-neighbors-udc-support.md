The **December 1, 2025** alert explained that the **City Commission** would hold a **final public hearing on the Unified Development Code (UDC) update** on **Tuesday, December 2, 2025**, including a **zoning map amendment** for the **Centennial Park** area within NENA. The message summarized how proposed **R-A** zoning differed from automatic conversion to **R-C**, NENA’s prior endorsement, and ways to submit comment before the hearing.

Bozeman Ave**). **Crystal Alegria** from Bozeman’s **Extreme History Project** presented on partnership with MSU archaeology related to land on **E. Mendenhall** slated for development. Thank-yous in the message included **Wild Crumb** and **Mountains Walking**.

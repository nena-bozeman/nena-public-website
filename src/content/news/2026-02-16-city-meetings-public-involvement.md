The **February 16** neighborhood email highlighted several City advisory meetings and projects that week, including:

- **Bozeman Creek Vision Plan** kickoff (**Tuesday, February 17**, noon–1 p.m., City Hall Madison room), with background on updated plan goals and a hybrid option to join. Project hub: [Bozeman Creek Vision Plan](https://engage.bozeman.net/bzncreek).
- **Bozeman City Study Commission** (**Wednesday, February 18**, 4–6 p.m.) on charter topics including the mayor structure and mayor–city manager relationship. [Bozeman City Study Commission](https://www.bozeman.net/services/bozeman-city-study-commission).
- **Historic Preservation Advisory Board** (**Wednesday, February 18**, 6–8 p.m.) with an NCOD-focused presentation and discussion of the design guidelines update engagement plan.
- **Tax Increment Financing Advisory Board** (**Thursday, February 19**, noon–1:30 p.m.) including Northeast Urban Renewal District work plan discussion.

Neighbors were also reminded about the **Neighborhood Traffic Calming Program** for street or intersection safety concerns: [Traffic calming](https://www.bozeman.net/departments/transportation-engineering/traffic-calming).

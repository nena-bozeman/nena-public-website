## NENA Town Hall: Cloverleaf Development Update

## Thursday, February 23, from 6:00 to 7:00 PM

  
Outlaw Partners presented information related to their new concept plans for the 2-acre lot owned by Bronken's Distributing on Thursday, February 23, 2023 during a NENA Town Hall.   
  
The Outlaw Partners are proposing 46 townhomes and 64 apartments on the 2-acre lot for a total of 110 units.  
  
They were given approval for 2.2 million in TIF funds to help build infrastructure in exchange for the promise of 14 affordable units and a height restriction to 4 stories.

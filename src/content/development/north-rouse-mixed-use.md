A proposed mixed-use development at the corner of North Rouse Avenue and Peach Street. The project includes 24 residential units (4 designated affordable) and approximately 3,200 sq ft of ground-floor retail space. The building's 4-story massing has raised concerns from neighboring property owners regarding scale and parking.

NENA's Development Watch Committee is tracking this project closely.

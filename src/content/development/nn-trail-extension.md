The City of Bozeman Parks Department has proposed an extension of the existing trail network to create a continuous multi-use path connecting the Northeast Neighborhood to Story Mill Community Park to the north. The project is in early planning stages with community input being sought.

NENA's Trails & Pocket Parks Committee is actively engaged in the planning process.

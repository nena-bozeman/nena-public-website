One of Bozeman's most beloved summer traditions! The Parade of Sheds is a self-guided tour of remarkable backyard sheds, studios, chicken coops, workshops, and outbuildings throughout the Northeast Neighborhood.

Maps available at Beall Park starting at 11 AM.
